- **Venue Description:**  
  924 Gilman Street, often simply referred to as "Gilman," is a renowned all-ages, non-profit, DIY music venue located in the West Berkeley neighborhood. Established in 1986, it has become a cornerstone of the punk rock community, known for its commitment to promoting alternative music and providing a space for emerging and underground bands. The venue operates with a strong ethos centered around inclusivity, affordability, and community involvement.
