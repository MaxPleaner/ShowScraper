## Overview

The Gray Area Cultural Incubator Showcase 2025 features a diverse range of digital and electronic art performances. The event will be held at Grey Area, a venue known for its intimate setting and focus on digital arts, with a capacity that accommodates a mix of seated and standing attendees. The atmosphere is typically one of engaged, passive listening.
