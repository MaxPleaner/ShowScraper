## Overview

The Gray Area Cultural Incubator Showcase 2025 will feature a diverse range of experimental and digital art performances. The event will take place at Grey Area, a venue known for its intimate setting and focus on digital and interactive art, typically accommodating a crowd that engages in passive listening and interactive participation. More details about the event can be found on the [event page](https://grayarea.org/event/cultural-incubator-showcase-2025/).
